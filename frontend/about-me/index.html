Hello! My name is Fredrikke and I am an Interaction design student at Kristiania. An early fascination with the service profession led me to start working in the hotel industry after living in Hong Kong. After several years in the leading hotels in Norway, I felt the need to combine service with something creative. Many small decisions led me to finally make a U-turn and have found my professional calling: UI/UX design. I have a trained eye in customer service and understanding the need of the customer. I always bring this knowledge to the table when working on a project.
